Info

  • Nen

    Nen: The technique that allows hunters to use their life energy. It is a powerful ability that includes several techniques, such as enhancement, manipulation, and transmutation.

  • Hunter Exam

    The Hunter Exam: A grueling test to earn a Hunter License. The exam is known for its physical and mental challenges and includes multiple rounds of tests, each designed to test different qualities of a potential Hunter.

  • Greed Island

    Greed Island: A game world that can lead to great wealth, but also grave danger. The game was created by hunters for other hunters. It is a treasure hunt set in an alternate world where players use Nen abilities to overcome challenges and find valuable treasures.

  • Chimera Ants

    The Chimera Ants are a deadly species of mutant ants with the ability to evolve and grow in strength by consuming other creatures. They are responsible for the near-destruction of humanity, which sets off a major plot point in the series.

Bounties

  • Phantom Troupe

    The Phantom Troupe is a group of thieves notorious for committing horrific crimes, including the massacre of an entire city. Each member has a high bounty, but they are extremely dangerous and difficult to apprehend.

  • Chrollo Lucilfer

    Chrollo Lucilfer is the leader of the Phantom Troupe. Known for his strategic mind and ability to steal and use other people's Nen abilities, he is an incredibly dangerous individual.

  • Hisoka

    Hisoka is a dangerous and sadistic former member of the Phantom Troupe. He is a master of combat and Nen and has an unpredictable nature, making him a high-priority target for bounty hunters.

  • Kurapika

    Kurapika is a skilled hunter and the last surviving member of the Kurta Clan. His primary goal is to avenge his family by hunting down the Phantom Troupe, who massacred his people to steal their valuable Scarlet Eyes.

Active Hunters

  • Gon Freecss

    Gon Freecss is a passionate and determined young hunter. He embarks on a journey to find his father, Ging Freecss, who abandoned him as a child. Gon is known for his incredible strength, optimism, and unwavering determination.

  • Killua Zoldyck

    Killua Zoldyck is a skilled assassin and the youngest member of the infamous Zoldyck Family. Despite his upbringing, he is fiercely loyal to his friends, especially Gon, and is known for his lightning-fast reflexes and Nen abilities.

  • Kurapika

    Kurapika is a member of the Kurta Clan and the last survivor of his people. He is driven by the desire to avenge his clan's massacre by the Phantom Troupe, and he is incredibly skilled in Nen techniques.

  • Leorio Paradinight

    Leorio Paradinight is a young and ambitious hunter who initially aims to become a doctor. Though his skills may not be as impressive as others, he is brave, loyal, and values the welfare of his friends.
